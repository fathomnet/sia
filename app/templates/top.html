Create an image classifer in four easy steps!

Step 1:

Create a Dataset by uploading a zip or tar file of images.

Step 2:

Step 3:

Create a classifier from the keyword against your dataset.

Step 4:

Iteratively improve your classifier by dividing the proposed image patches into correct and incorrect groups.
